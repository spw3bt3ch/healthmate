About HealthMate

HealthMate is a comprehensive health awareness application designed to help users calculate and monitor various health indicators, such as:

  • Body Mass Index (BMI)
  • Waist-to-Hip Ratio (WHR)
  • Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)
  • Stroke Risk Assessment
  • Arthritis Risk Estimation

This application is intended for educational and informational purposes only. It does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the guidance of your doctor or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, easy-to-use tools that promote self-awareness and encourage healthy lifestyle choices.
